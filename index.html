About

Welcome to Kerin’s Tiny Guest Suite — a cozy hideaway nestled beneath a grand oak tree in Lodi, California. Designed for simplicity, comfort, and charm, our tiny home is perfect for travelers seeking relaxation and a taste of wine country life. Whether you’re here for a weekend getaway or exploring the region’s vineyards, you’ll find everything you need for a peaceful stay.

Things to Do in Lodi

Lodi Lake & Boathouse

Rent a kayak or paddleboard, stroll the nature trails, or enjoy a picnic by the water.

Harney Lane Vineyards

Visit this popular winery for award-winning wines and a relaxed outdoor setting.

Micke Grove Park & Zoo

Explore gardens, the zoo, and the peaceful Japanese tea garden — perfect for families and couples alike.

Historic Downtown Lodi

Stroll under the Lodi Arch, visit local boutiques, and enjoy farm-to-table dining experiences.
